Tips for Success

Beginner Tips

Take your time to analyze each puzzle before making moves. Look for patterns and try to plan a few steps ahead for better results.

Advanced Strategies

Focus on resource management and prioritize survival over speed. Sometimes taking a defensive approach is better than rushing forward.

Resource Management

Don't use all your resources at once. Save some for critical situations and unexpected challenges that may appear later.

Practice Makes Perfect

Don't get discouraged by failures. Each attempt teaches you something new about the game mechanics and puzzle patterns.
